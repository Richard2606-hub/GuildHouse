# LectureForge Pedagogy Guidelines
Version: 1.2
Source: GuildHouse Internal Education Standards

## Flashcard Generation Standards
- Goal: Active recall testing.
- Format: Front should be a single, unambiguous question or prompt. Back should be a concise, definitive answer.
- Fact-Locking: Every flashcard MUST be directly sourced from the provided lecture transcript or slides. Do not introduce external facts even if they are factually correct.
- Anti-Pattern: Avoid "What is [X] and why is it important?" (Too complex for one card). Split into two cards.

## Summary Generation Standards
- Goal: Quick review and mental mapping.
- Format: Bulleted list with hierarchical indentation. 
- Maximum length: 500 words per 30 minutes of lecture.
- Tone: Academic, objective, concise. No conversational filler like "In this lecture, the professor talked about..."

## Practice Question Standards
- Goal: Assessing comprehension and application.
- Format: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) with 4 options (A, B, C, D).
- Distractors: Incorrect options must be plausible but definitively wrong based *only* on the provided lecture material.
- Explanation: The answer key must explain *why* the correct answer is right, citing the specific concept from the lecture.
